Add My Acharya Info

Help preserve and enrich Acharya traditions by submitting authentic information about your Acharya.

Information submitted through this form will be manually reviewed before being added to Arulicheyal.org.

What Information Can Be Submitted?

1. Acharya Name
Official Tamil name of the Acharya.

2. Tamil Month of Birth
Example: Chithirai, Vaikasi, Aippasi, etc.

3. Thirunatchathiram
Example: Moolam, Swathi, Rohini, etc.

4. Thaniyan
Complete Thaniyan text if available.

5. Vazhi Thirunamam
Complete Vazhi Thirunamam text.

6. Thiru Naalpattu
Full text if available.

7. Thongal
Full text if available.

8. Supporting References
PDF, image, book scan, temple publication or mutt publication.

Review Process

Submission
↓
Manual Verification
↓
Database Review
↓
Publication on Arulicheyal.org
All submissions are manually reviewed. Submission does not guarantee publication. Supporting references in the form of image are must to verify the authenticity.
